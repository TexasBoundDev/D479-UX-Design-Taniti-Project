Lodging Information

Resort name: Coral Haven Resort & Spa

Description: Located along Taniti's peaceful eastern coastline, Coral Haven Resort & Spa offers guests a relaxing tropical escape surrounded by crystal-clear waters and lush island scenery. The resort combines modern comfort with traditional Tanitian design, providing visitors with a luxurious beachfront experience while allowing them to connect with the island's natural beauty and culture.

Price: Standard rooms: $250 per night
Ocean-view suites: $425 per night
Private villas: $700 per night

Amenities: Private beachfront access
Ocean-view rooms and suites
Infinity pool overlooking the Pacific Ocean
Full-service spa and wellness center
On-site restaurant featuring local Tanitian cuisine
Complimentary breakfast
Guided hiking and cultural tours
Snorkeling and water activity rentals
Airport transportation services
Free Wi-Fi throughout the resort
